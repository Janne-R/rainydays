About us

History

Our story started in 2020 and Rainydays is founded by a local woman who saw the need for quality rain jackets for the active man and women without the high cost.

Location

We are a online store based in the rainy city of Bergen, Norway.

Products

Rainydays are selling men’s and woman’s rain jackets. Our Rain jackets a designed for a range of different outdoors activities such as hiking, exploring, skiing, camping and canoeing.

Price

Our jackets are mid-range in price and emphasize durability and being suitable for a range of different weather types ensuring customers are comfortable whatever adventure they go on.
